About Us

Our Mission

Dream Nest is a magazine that informs and provides a service to occupiers of space in their early 20’s to mid 30’s on how to use minimalist style to create a trendy, accessible, and sustainable space.

A little about our mission and purpose

Dream Nest focuses on home design in a fun, accessible, collaborative, and sustainable way. We appeal to smaller spaces for younger people in urban cities. We recognize our audience as space occupiers in a difficult real estate market whose funds are limited. Females in their early 20’s to mid 30’s with lower income but are educated and value aesthetically pleasing environments. We make beautiful home design possible within the parameters of being a millennial. Price is major consideration we take into account when imagining our content so readers can create a space that can be achieved by themselves. Through image and editorial content readers will develop a unique and affordable aesthetic using elements from the magazine to gain inspiration on design, home renovation, and decluttering. Come dream with us!

A little about our business

Dream Nest publishes a monthly print edition and a daily digital edition. Our 48 page print edition will be distributed to subscribers, newsstands, and local shops; commercial and private. On our website, we will have content being produced daily. Our newsletter subscribers will receive exclusive content and can contribute to our social media and online content sharing with what they have done with their own spaces that inspire us!

Marketing plan

Our target market is women between the ages of 20-35 who are interested inaffordable home design as well as all aspects of design, livability, and sustainability.Our audience will be living in a cosy space in a largely populated city and looking for doable inspiration on trends and ideas that started as a daydream. The majority of our marketing will take place online and through the hot-spots we have researched(coffee shops, galleries, tea houses, furniture/decor stores, etc.) where our demographic will be according to Vividata and Simply Analytics. Social media will be the largest push for marketing as our audience spends most of their free time on their platforms.

Management & Financial Plan

Dream Nest is a profit magazine that will aim to grow financially each year of activity. We will start out regionally with the intention to go nationally with the magazine. We will use our profit to bring our magazine to all provinces and territories and help grow the diversity of our content and reach. We will aim to target the larger cities of Canada, first beginning in Vancouver, British Columbia and branching to other large cities such as Montreal, Quebec and Toronto, Ontario. Our profitable model will allow us to have a larger distribution and staffing for our magazine and help us travel to the different provinces to promote our mission of a stylish and affordable space for our young demographic. We will gain our finances through our magazines sales in subscription and single copy sales and through our advertising strategy.
